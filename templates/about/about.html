About Ojukaye

Connecting Nigeria's voices, sharing authentic stories, and delivering trusted news

Our Mission

Ojukaye was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a platform where Nigerians can share their stories, engage in meaningful discussions, and access reliable news from trusted sources.

We believe in the power of community-driven content combined with professional journalism to create an informed, engaged, and connected society.

Our Story

Ojukaye began in 2024 as a response to the growing need for a platform that combines social engagement with reliable news delivery. Founded by a team of Nigerian journalists and tech entrepreneurs, we set out to create something different.

Today, we've grown into a vibrant community of writers, journalists, business owners, and everyday Nigerians sharing their perspectives and staying informed about the issues that matter.
